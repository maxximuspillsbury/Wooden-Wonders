Our Maine Story

Based in the heart of rural Maine, Wooden Wonders is a family-owned business dedicated to old-world craftsmanship. We don't just build sheds; we build "Wonders."


Every Hobbit Hole is meticulously designed and handcrafted by our skilled artisans, ensuring your structure is as durable as it is enchanting. From the iconic round doors to premium cedar finishes, we bring fantasy to life with quality that lasts a lifetime.
